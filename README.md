## 📌 Project Overview

A retail Superstore is experiencing **inconsistent profitability** across regions, product categories, and customer segments — with some areas running at a **loss**.

This project analyzes **~10,000 rows** of sales data to:
- 🔍 Identify **loss-making areas**
- 📊 Understand the **impact of discounts** on profit
- 💡 Provide **data-driven recommendations** to improve overall profitability

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions

| # | Question |
|---|----------|
| 1 | Which **regions** are profitable and which are losing money? |
| 2 | Which **product categories/sub-categories** are driving losses? |
| 3 | Which **customer segments** are most and least profitable? |
| 4 | Are **discounts** causing products to become unprofitable? |
| 5 | What is the **relationship between discount level and profit**? |
| 6 | What **actionable steps** can improve overall profitability? |

---

## 🗂️ Dataset

- **Source:** [Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)
- **Rows:** ~10,000
- **Columns:** 21 (including Order Date, Region, Category, Sales, Discount, Profit, etc.)
- **File:** [`Superstore_Sales.csv`](./data/Superstore_Sales.csv)
